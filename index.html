01 / CONCEPT

The Psychology of Space

Parallax design leverages standard ocular physics to simulate optical displacement. By scaling the lateral speed of page elements based on their virtual depth, our human eye translates this speed contrast into physical volume.

The static structures elements move slowly, while items near the front pass by your vision swiftly. When layered, these simple transitions translate a flat monitor interface into a dynamic space worth exploring.
